Free coffee

In our Cafeteria you can buy a ‘Caffè sospeso’, which means a suspended or pended coffee. The term originates from an Italian tradition which means that you can buy an extra cup of coffee that the coffee shop will then give, for free, to anyone who comes in the shop who is down on their luck and can’t afford to pay for a cup of coffee.
